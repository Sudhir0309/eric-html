Senior UI Developer

I've been passionate about user interfaces since I was a child. Over the years, this turned into a real love for designing intuative layouts.

After mastering the art of creating large, scalable, and busy user interfaces, the complexity and challenges of designing interfaces for big enterprise applications had become something I've enjoyed.

I’m an excellent UX Designer too

UX Designer

After spending 10 years as a UI developer, I decided to dive into UX design. I enrolled in the prestigious Interaction Design Foundation where I earned six certifications.

Three years into practicing design, I developed a deeper passion for usability. It was the perfect blend of my UI development experience, design skills, and my desire to create custom user-centric solutions.
